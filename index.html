A Poem for Nagham

In a garden where the roses bloom,
Lives a girl with a heart of gold,
Her name is Nagham, a sweet tune,
A story of love and joy untold.

With eyes that sparkle like the stars,
And a smile that lights up the night,
Nagham dances under the moon,
Her spirit shining so bright.

Her laughter is the melody,
That fills the world with cheer,
In her presence, hearts are warm,
She is cherished, she is dear.

Oh Nagham, you're a symphony,
Of grace, of hope, of dreams,
May your days be ever joyful,
Flowing like gentle streams.
